## How It Works

1. **Upload your resume** — InterviewAce learns your actual background, not generic talking points
2. **Paste the job description** — suggestions are tailored to the specific role
3. **Start your interview** — InterviewAce listens via system audio and classifies each question in real time

## The Ethics Question

The most common objection: is this cheating?

Interviews have always rewarded preparation. AI copilots shift the *type* of preparation — you still need a real work history, genuine stories, and role-specific context. The copilot helps you access that preparation under the stress conditions of a live interview. Think of it as notes in an open-book exam: they don't replace knowledge, they scaffold recall.

Candidates who haven't prepared get little from InterviewAce. There's nothing in their background worth surfacing.
